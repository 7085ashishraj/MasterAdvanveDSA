Product of natural numbers 

Difficulty: EasyMax Score: 25
25 Points
Problem Description
Create a recursive program to compute the product of all natural numbers up to K. Write a recursive function that calculates and returns the product of all natural numbers from K down to 1.

Input Format

Enter an integer K (K > 0).

Output Format

Print the product of all natural numbers from K down to 1 (both included).


Example
Example 1

Input
3

Output
6

Explanation
3 * 2 * 1 = 6


Example 2

Input
6

Output
720

Explanation
6 * 5 * 4 * 3 * 2 * 1 = 720


Problem Constraints
1 <= N <= 14


Topics
Recursion
